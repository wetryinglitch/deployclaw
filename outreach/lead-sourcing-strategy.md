# DeployClaw — Lead Sourcing Strategy

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- **Company size**: 4-50 employees
- **Stage**: Seed to Series B (funded, some budget)
- **Roles to target**: CEO, CTO, COO, Head of Sales, Head of Ops
- **Industries**: SaaS, fintech, e-commerce, agencies, consulting, real estate, legal
- **Signals**: Talking about AI, productivity, automation, hiring pain, email overload
- **Geography**: US-first, then expand (remote deployment = anywhere)

## Data Sources for Lead Lists

### 1. Crunchbase
- Filter: 4-50 employees, Seed-Series B, funded in last 12 months
- Export founder names + emails
- URL: crunchbase.com/search/organizations

### 2. Y Combinator Directory
- Current + recent batches (W25, S24, W24)
- These are tech-savvy, resource-constrained, perfect ICP
- URL: ycombinator.com/companies

### 3. Product Hunt
- Companies that launched AI/productivity tools
- Founders are already AI-forward thinkers
- URL: producthunt.com

### 4. LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Search: CEO/Founder/CTO, company size 1-50, industry: Technology
- Filter by recent posts about AI, productivity, hiring
- Export to CSV

### 5. Twitter/X Lists
- Follow lists of startup founders
- Monitor keywords: "AI assistant", "email overload", "hiring EA", "productivity hack", "OpenClaw", "ClawdBot"
- Engage first, then DM

### 6. AngelList / Wellfound
- Startup directory with employee count filters
- URL: wellfound.com/discover/startups

### 7. Apollo.io / Hunter.io
- Email finder + enrichment
- Build targeted lists with verified emails
- Integrate with cold email tool

### 8. Reddit
- r/startups, r/SaaS, r/entrepreneur, r/selfhosted
- Find people asking about AI assistants, email automation
- Engage in comments, soft pitch

### 9. Indie Hackers
- Founders building solo or small teams
- Perfect for single-agent deployment
- URL: indiehackers.com

### 10. SetupClaw's Own Social Proof
- Look at who's commenting/engaging with @michael_chomsky on Twitter
- These people are already warm to the concept
- Approach with "same service, 20% less"

## Lead Enrichment Stack
1. **Find leads**: Crunchbase, YC, LinkedIn
2. **Get emails**: Apollo.io or Hunter.io
3. **Verify emails**: NeverBounce or ZeroBounce
4. **Enrich data**: Clearbit or Apollo
5. **Send cold emails**: Instantly.ai or Smartlead
6. **Send cold DMs**: Manual or semi-automated via LinkedIn/Twitter skills
7. **Cold calls**: Bland AI (already set up)

## Outreach Cadence
- **Day 0**: Cold email #1 (or cold DM)
- **Day 2**: LinkedIn connection request
- **Day 3**: Cold email #2 (value add)
- **Day 5**: LinkedIn DM follow-up
- **Day 7**: Cold email #3 (FOMO)
- **Day 10**: Cold call via Bland AI
- **Day 14**: Break-up email ("last note")

## Weekly Targets
- 200 cold emails sent
- 50 LinkedIn connections + DMs
- 20 Twitter DMs
- 10 cold calls
- Goal: 5-10 booked calls/week → 2-3 closes/month
